Welcome to the small app i have designed

Here is a list of the options available for you:

  • root
    - will show you the list in the order i received it from the API
  • products/?
    - ? indicates page number. Shows 10 items from the list ordered by price. No argument will give the full list instead of 10 items
  • /products/kids/?
    - ? indicates page number. Show 10 items from the list that has kids = 1 ordered by price. No argument will give the full list instead of 10 items
  • /products/id/?
    - ? indicates id number or productID can be found by both id's. Show the items you have searched for. No argument will raise 404
  • /admin
    - Use the SuperAdmin user/pass you created the database with for an administrative platform, allowing you to manage the list
